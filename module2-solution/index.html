Spinach Pie

Albanian spinach pie is a savory dish made with flaky baked phyllo dough layers filled with spinach, feta cheese, eggs and herbs for flavor. Byrek is traditionally made with several phyllo dough sheets that have been thinly rolled out by hand. It can also be made with ready made pre-rolled phyllo pastry sheets.
Have you tried? You can replace spinach with large amounts of fresh basil and parsley, finely chopped.

Legume

Legume Soup is a traditional Albanian red bean soup that has a variable recipe (and even name), depending on the region of the country. The preparation starts by soaking the red beans overnight, after which they are rinsed and boiled. Meanwhile, diced onions are fried in olive oil until golden-brown, to which tomato sauce, parsley, and some of the bean stock are added.
Once the sauce has thickened, it is combined with the boiling beans.
